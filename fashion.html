Fashion Section

Woven Division

The tower floors at Hemayetpur, Savar, and the LEED-Gold Eco-sustainable green premise at Dhamrai, Dhaka, are exclusively dedicated for the woven division of the group.

Fabrics

The AKH procures woven fabrics of highest standard and as per the buyers’ requirements. As well, these fabrics are procured from its own sources meticulously chosen based on the track record of repute in maintaining delivery schedule and quality specifications.

Inspecting thoroughly yard-by-yard conforming to the 4-point checking system, woven division of AKH issues fabrics to the production processes.

Woven Sample & Design Department

Staffed by highly skilled and experienced master tailors, AKH woven division’s sample section is meant for exact reproduction of approved samples in virtually no time at all.

Caption: To tune very carefully the production orchestration beforehand, it holds trial production meeting and process invariably.

Knit Division

The knit division of AKH is completely vertical. To support its three apparel units, it has got knitting and other facilities necessary to feed itself for its entire export demands.

Caption: Subsequently, AKH knit division tunes very carefully the production orchestration beforehand thru a pre-production meeting and a successful trial production rehearsal.

Knitting

The knit wing of AKH produces all of the knit fabrics it needs to meet its entire export demand. Using the world’s latest and most dependable Europe-origin Knitting machines like Mayer & C, Origio and Bigoni etc, AKH manufactures numerous varieties of knit fabrics including single and double jersey, ribs, any elastane, drop ribs, interlock, PKs, engineered stripe, terry brush back fleece, sweat, using any kind of blends like cotton, polyester, cotton-poly, modal, viscose, poly viscose, tensil, coolmax, and any fine fabric weighing 70-80 grams per square meter, etc. At present, it is producing about 18 tons of knit fabrics every month.

Fleece Apparel

AKH Stitch Art, a concern of AKH group, however, is exclusively dedicated for producing knitted fleece apparel. On its 126,000 square feet of floor space, about 2000 skilled people now produce about 400,000 pieces of diverse fleece items of superb quality, every month. QC operation here conforms to the group norms.
